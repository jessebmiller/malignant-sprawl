# Malignant Sprawl

A tabletop game of megastructure exploration for 2-5 PCs and 1 GM.

## Before play starts

Choose or create a megastructure playbook. There are a few included here as
well as advice for creating your own.

One player, probably you if you're introducing it to your friends, will
play the Megastructure Character (MC). 

The other players will play Explorer Characters (EC).

The object of the game is to understand the megastructure through the eyes
of the explorers.

## Introduction

The MC, following the chosen Megastructure Playbook, introduces the
megastructure.

The ECs, following the Explorer Playbook, introduce their explorer.

## Explore 

The MC orients the explorers to the megastructure. Using the Megastructure
Playbook as inspiration to describe details of the place, the situation,
people, creatures, or beings. Set the scene.

Exploration leads to an encounter when an explorer notices something important
or that they are curious about.

## Encounters

Use the moves in your playbooks to play encounters.

Encounters lead to either more exploration, or to danger.

### Missing something imortant

When playing encounters, some moves may grant the MC "missed something
important tokens" (MSIT). They may spend an MSIT on one of the following moves.

- Put them in sudden danger
- Reveal a concerning fact, contradictory evidence
- Surprise them with something they missed
