# Explorer

You are exploring a megastructure.

## Introduction

In each round below, each explorer answers, simply, the questions in the round
before moving on to later rounds.

### Round one

- Are you exploring by choice or by nessecity?

### Round two

- Who or what has the megastructure taken from you? How?
- What do you believe about the megastructure that few take seriously?

### Round three

For each other explorer, ask them:

- What have you seen me do that is admirable?
- How do I frustrate, annoy, or trouble you?
- What lie do I believe about the megastructure?
